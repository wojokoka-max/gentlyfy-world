# Claude Project Rules

## General Rules

- Do not redesign the platform philosophy.
- Do not introduce social media mechanics.
- Do not add dopamine-driven engagement systems.
- Do not create endless feeds.
- Do not add aggressive notifications.
- Do not overcomplicate UX.

## Development Rules

- Work slowly and modularly.
- Present plans before implementing features.
- Preserve emotional pacing.
- Prioritize atmosphere over optimization.
- Every interaction should feel calm and intentional.

## Design Rules

- Soft transitions only.
- Low stimulation UI.
- Emotional safety first.
- Silence is part of UX.
- Space and breathing room matter.

## Technical Rules

- Keep architecture modular.
- Avoid unnecessary complexity.
- Build experience prototypes before scaling.
- Test emotional UX before feature expansion.
