# Calm Spaces

Calm Spaces are not content categories.

They are emotional digital environments.

Examples:
- Rain Space,
- Soft Night,
- Calm Evening,
- Recovery Space,
- Slow Sunday,
- Cozy Kitchen.

Features may include:
- ambient visuals,
- breathing guidance,
- calming sounds,
- emotional atmosphere,
- gentle recommendations,
- slow interaction.

Purpose:
Create emotional regulation and safe digital presence.
