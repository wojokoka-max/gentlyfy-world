# Gentlyfy Experience Bible

Gentlyfy is a digital emotional house.

It is not designed for speed, productivity or endless consumption.

The platform should feel:
- warm,
- atmospheric,
- emotionally safe,
- sensory-friendly,
- curiosity-driven.

Every room has:
- its own emotional energy,
- lighting,
- pacing,
- atmosphere,
- interaction style,
- sound identity,
- seasonal feeling.

Navigation should feel like:
- walking through spaces,
- discovering rooms,
- opening doors,
- entering atmospheres.

Never create:
- pressure,
- urgency,
- overstimulation,
- social competition.

The platform should emotionally regulate the nervous system through:
- pacing,
- visuals,
- silence,
- movement,
- warmth,
- emotional atmosphere.
